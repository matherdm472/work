Welcome to
The daily celebrity tweet guessing game

Tweedle updates at midnight
In Tweedle, your goal is to guess which celebrity made certain tweets. You have 5 chances to get it right, or you lose the game. The clues will get easier as you progress.
Instructions:
1. Read the tweet and try to guess which celebrity tweeted it.
2. Type in the name of the celebrity you think is the author of the tweet and click on the "Search" button.
3. If your guess is correct, you win the game!
4. If your guess is wrong, you lose an attempt.
5. Click on the menu button to explore previous games.
Don't forget to explore the settings for additional challenges.
